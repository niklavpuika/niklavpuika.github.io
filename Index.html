About Niklāvs Zahars

Niklāvs Zahars is an artist, residing in Liepāja. Currently he is working on an exhibition with a colaboration with another artist. He uses techniques like colored pencils and acrilic paint, but also using those together.

Niklāvs is originally from a small village named Cīrava and moved to Liepāja with his mom since there were more opportunities to succeed in life. Some of the hobbies of the artist include
  • Painting/drawing
  • Reading
  • Travelling
  • Spending time with friends and family
  • Learning new languages
